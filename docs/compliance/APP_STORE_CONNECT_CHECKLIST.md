## Review notes (paste)

```
Screel is a screen-time / digital-wellbeing helper.

Users set a daily minute allowance and may authorize Apple Family Controls / DeviceActivity / ManagedSettings for selected apps. When the allowance is empty, those apps can be shielded.

Optional minigames award a FIXED number of minutes on success (daily cap). There is no wagering, no stake, and no payout that deducts from the user’s bank on a loss. Misses award nothing and leave the bank unchanged.

Not simulated gambling. No real money, deposits, withdrawals, or cash prizes.

18+ age gate. United States only. No demo account — all data on-device.
```
