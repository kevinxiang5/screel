Privacy Policy

Screel (“we”) provides a screen-time helper app. Contact: support@screel.app (replace with your real support email).

Data on your device

We store a display nickname, minute bank, focus goal and setup answers, challenge history, and settings locally on your device. v1 has no account system.

Network

The web build may load fonts from Google Fonts. No analytics or advertising SDKs ship in the current app. On iOS, if you connect Screen Time, Apple Family Controls / Device Activity APIs process app-selection and usage signals on-device to enforce your minute bank (shields). That data is not uploaded to Screel servers (v1 has none).

Children

Screel is not directed to children under 13 (age floor subject to counsel confirmation).

Your choices

Clear app/site storage or reinstall to erase local data. Email support for privacy requests.
