Privacy Policy

Screel (“we”) provides a screen-time helper app. Contact: support@screel.app.

Data on your device

We store a display nickname, minute bank, focus goal and setup answers, challenge history, and settings locally on your device. v1 has no account system.

Network

The web build may load fonts from Google Fonts. On iOS, if you connect Screen Time, Apple Family Controls / Device Activity APIs process app-selection and usage signals on-device to enforce your minute bank (shields). That data is not uploaded to Screel servers.

Rewarded advertising

Normal users may choose to view a rewarded ad supplied by Google AdMob. Screel requests non-personalized ads. Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, IP address, ad interactions, diagnostics, and related advertising data to deliver ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. See Google’s Privacy Policy. Premium users are not shown ads.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Screel receives entitlement status and transaction information needed to unlock Premium; payment details are handled by Apple.

Children

Screel is not directed to children under 13 (age floor subject to counsel confirmation).

Your choices

Clear app/site storage or reinstall to erase local data. Email support for privacy requests.
